Using the Computer for Research

The computer has been a great gamechanger in doing research.

You can use the internet and the computer to further your research

It can be helpful both for taking notes and finding information

Getting Going

Taking Notes

Bookmarks and documents with notes are great for organizing research. You should take notes where you find good information. It will help you if you need to reference the material for yourself or make a bibliography.

Finding Web Pages

You can find web pages through google. You can also find links from websites. Say you are looking for information about marine mammasl, you can go to the aquariums website and find many links.

Using the Library's Catalog

You can use the library's catalog much in the same way you use google. You can also search by the self to see what items are near a book at the library online. This will help you find nonfiction books on a similar topic (or fiction books by the same author).
